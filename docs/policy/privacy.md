# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2025-12-10_

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information
about users who access the Service, including when they sign in with
third-party OAuth providers (such as Google or Meta).

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you sign in to the Service, we receive limited profile information
from your chosen OAuth provider. Depending on the provider and your
settings, this may include:

- Email address
- Name or display name
- Profile image URL
- Provider-specific user identifier

We may also store basic technical information generated when you use
the Service, such as:

- Log timestamps (sign-in, sign-out, errors)
- IP address and browser information

---

## 2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

- Authenticate users and provide secure access to the Service
- Maintain user accounts and basic profiles
- Improve reliability, security, and performance of the Service
- Investigate and prevent fraud, abuse, or technical issues

We do not use your personal information for advertising or for
unrelated marketing activities.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the
following limited situations:

- With service providers who help us operate the Service
	(for example, cloud hosting or logging), under appropriate
	confidentiality and security obligations
- When required by law, regulation, or legal process

OAuth providers (such as Google or Meta) may independently collect and
process data under their own privacy policies. Please review their
policies for more details.

---

## 4. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as
needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations.

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize
personal information within a reasonable period, except where we are
required to retain it under applicable laws.
